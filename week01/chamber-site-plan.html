Silang Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The name of the site would be "Silang Chamber of Commerce Site Plan". I chose this site name to emphasis the talents and help the people in Silang to grow their source of income.

Our Purpose

Silang Chamber of Commerce will help the city of Silang to grow economically and to assist the farmers to promote their products through show casing it in different social media platforms.

Scenarios

  • What type of products will be posted or promote in this chamber?
  • What are the social media platforms where the business will be promoted?
  • Where can I find the contact information of chamber's owner?
